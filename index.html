Hi, I'm Yicheng Su :)

Biochemist, aspiring neuroscientist

I am in the final year of my integrated Master’s in Biochemistry at the University of Oxford. My biochemistry training has given me a strong appreciation for chemical signals and their role in biological systems. Alongside this, I have built a growing computational skill set through bioinformatics and machine learning. Looking ahead, I am eager to deepen my understanding of electrical signals, which I see as highly complementary to chemical and computational perspectives. By bringing these three dimensions together, I aim to develop new tools and frameworks for decoding memory, emotion, and other aspects of human experience. My curiosity spans memory, sleep, dreams, and emotions, as well as how altered states — including those induced by psychedelics — reshape the brain’s activity and perception.
