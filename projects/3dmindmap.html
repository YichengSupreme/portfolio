3D Mind Map

Interactive 3D visualization map

Project Overview

I am developing a 3D mind mapping application to visualize and navigate complex relationships between ideas. Unlike traditional note-taking tools, this system allows information to be arranged in an infinite 3D space and then sliced into 2D views for focused exploration. My motivation came from biochemistry, where understanding requires mapping interconnected pathways and proteins, but I believe the same approach can support any field that relies on recognizing patterns across large networks of knowledge. Importantly, our thoughts themselves are not two-dimensional, and I want to create tools that better reflect this richness.

Building this tool has allowed me to experiment with database design, 3D visualization, and user interaction. In the longer term, I aim to incorporate it into VR, so that users can be immersed in their own knowledge graphs. I see this project as a step toward more immersive ways of capturing and exploring scientific knowledge — an interest I hope to expand through neuroscience and human–computer interaction.
