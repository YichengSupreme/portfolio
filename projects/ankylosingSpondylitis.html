Ankylosing Spondylitis Research

Multi-omics analysis of a chronic inflammatory disease affecting 1 in 200 people

Project Overview

For my Master's project in Dr Julian Knight lab, I am analysing omics datasets, including ATAC-seq, mRNA-seq, ChIP-seq, and Chromosomal Conformation Capture, to study Ankylosing Spondylitis, a chronic inflammatory disease that affects every 1 in 200 people.

This comprehensive multi-omics approach allows me to investigate the disease from multiple molecular perspectives, providing insights into the underlying mechanisms and potential therapeutic targets.
